Health Influencer Life Expectancy

Does diet affect longevity? A data visualization analysis.

About & Methodology

Selection Criteria

This dataset includes prominent health influencers, nutritionists, and bodybuilders who have authored books, had significant public followings, or made notable contributions to nutritional science. The list focuses on individuals who have passed away to analyze their life expectancy.

Categorization Logic

To simplify the analysis, diets were grouped into two broad categories:

  • Low Meat: Includes Vegans, Vegetarians, Plant-Based dieters, and those following Balanced/Whole Foods or Mediterranean diets where meat is not the primary focus.
  • High Meat: Includes Carnivore, Paleo, Keto, Low-Carb, and Bodybuilding diets where animal protein is a central component.

Data Processing

Individuals who died before the age of 30 were excluded from the main visualizations to remove outliers that might not be diet-related (e.g., accidents or congenital conditions), although they may still appear in the full list. Ages were verified against public records and obituaries.
